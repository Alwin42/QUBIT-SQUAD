Tech Fest by Qubit-Squad & CSE Department, AISAT College

About CyberForge

CyberForge is the annual tech fest organized by the Qubit-Squad and the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department of AISAT College. It is a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and innovation. The fest features a wide range of events, workshops, and competitions designed to inspire and challenge participants.

Events and Activities

  • Workshops: Hands-on sessions on cutting-edge technologies like AI, ML, IoT, and more.
  • Hackathons: Code, collaborate, and create innovative solutions to real-world problems.
  • Makathons: Build and present hardware-based projects in a competitive environment.
  • Technical Events: Coding challenges, quizzes, and debugging contests.
  • Guest Lectures: Insights from industry experts and tech leaders.

Organized By

CyberForge is proudly organized by the Qubit-Squad, a student-led tech community, and the CSE Department of AISAT College. The team is dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and learning among students.
